# Here's some extra info about Paper you might want to know!

1. Paper comes with a Config table where you can store default values.
2. Paper comes with built-in modules made by me such as Data, Network, and Signals.
3. Paper comes with an Assets folder that can be accessed very quick and easily.

### Paper Framework's 'Data' Page makes use of the ProfileStore module by loleris. All credits apply to them.
