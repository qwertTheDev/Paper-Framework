## Q. What is the point of this?
### A. The point of Paper isn't to be a new innovative way to develop games or compete with other frameworks, it is to improve your overall experience while using methods you may already find familiar.

## Q. Why not just use Knit or Flamework?
### A. You definitely can, but those are vastly different from the point of Paper Framework.

## Q. Is this open source?
### A. No, the source is very shy. But you can try taking a look.
